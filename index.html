Are you craving smoothies but also trying to save money or do not know how to make them? Not to worry, Smooth Tooth is here to provide a comprehensive guide to creating the perfect smoothie for you!

By the end of exploring all the contents Smooth Tooth has to offer, you'll have a full understanding of how the process of making smoothies works. You'll understand the proportions of creating different consistencies of smoothies. You'll learn the different nutritional benefits each type of ingredient provides in a smoothie. You'll learn different combinations of smoothie ingredients and eventually experiment with your own. Here, at Smooth Tooth, you are making smoothies not one specific smoothie.

The Smoothie lab page will give more explanation on different ingredient flavors and their prospective nutritional value. Learn different proportions between liquid and solid ingredients and different techniques on creating the perfect smoothie using the tools at your disposal. The Smoothie Tutorial page will give an example of a smoothie tutorial using fixed ingredients but will offer suggestions on how to make it different based on user preference.
