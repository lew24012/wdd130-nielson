Start with Right Tools

Start by finding the right tools and proportions for creating a smoothie. When creating our smoothies, we use a blender and not a food processor. A food processor will chop and slice ingredients more efficiently, but a blender will liquify them, creating the perfect smoothie consistency.

Other Ingredients Inside the Smoothie

The ideal smoothie is 2 parts fruit/vegetable, 1 part liquid, and Â½ part thickener. You can adjust the texture by adding more liquid (for a thinner smoothie) or more thickener (for a thicker smoothie). The flavor comes from your ingredients, so experiment with different combinations to find your favorite taste.

Sweeteners and Flavor Enhancers

To enhance the flavor, you can add honey, peanut butter, Greek yogurt, or syrup. Ice can also be used as a thickener. Frozen fruits work best as they blend more smoothly and help maintain the cold temperature of your smoothie. You can also add spices, crackers, chocolate, or any other ingredient to customize your smoothie.
